## 01 — The Manifesto

Reject the trend. *ट्रेंड को नकारो।*
Reject the algorithm. *एल्गोरिदम को नकारो।*
Reject performing. *दिखावे को नकारो।*
Reject the gaze. *नज़र को नकारो।*
Reject the label. *लेबल को नकारो।*
Reject everything that isn't you. *वो सब नकारो जो तुम नहीं हो।*

rejct.all is not a trend. It is not an aesthetic. It is not a core. It is not a moment on the internet that will be forgotten by Thursday.

It is a position. A position you wear on your body, carry in your posture, and hold in the way you move through a world that wants you to perform constantly — for the algorithm, for the gaze, for the idea of who you should be.

We exist for the generation that is done performing. Gen Z and Gen Alpha — the first two generations who grew up with the internet baked into their identity, who know what manufactured cool looks like, who are exhausted by it, and who are choosing, deliberately, to step out.

We are Indian. We speak Hindi and English — not one first, not one better. Both equal. Both always. This is not bilingual branding. This is who we are.

Genderless by design. Not as a trend. Not as a checkbox. Because the clothes do not care. The fabric does not care. Only you decide what it means when you wear it.

## 03 — The Six Rejections

1. **Reject the trend.** *ट्रेंड को नकारो।* — We do not chase what is popular this season. The drop exists because the design is right — not because it is timely.
2. **Reject the algorithm.** *एल्गोरिदम को नकारो।* — We are not building for reach. We are building for the person who finds us and immediately recognises themselves.
3. **Reject performing.** *दिखावे को नकारो।* — The clothes are not a costume. You wear rejct.all because you mean it. The embroidery is quiet. The statement is loud.
4. **Reject the gaze.** *नज़र को नकारो।* — We do not dress for approval. The garment is a decision made in private, worn in public, answering to no one.
5. **Reject the label.** *लेबल को नकारो।* — Men's. Women's. Indian. Western. Every label is a box. We build clothes that do not fit in boxes.
6. **Reject everything that isn't you.** *वो सब नकारो जो तुम नहीं हो।* — The final rejection. The most personal one. The one that takes the longest to arrive at. When you get there — you already know.

## 05 — Design Language (established in Drop 01, governs all future drops)

1. **Off-white as base.** The brand's skin. Default identity across every Drop 01 piece.
2. **Graphic as statement.** Never decoration. The graphic is the point; the garment is the vehicle.
3. **Wine red as primary embroidery colour.** Burgundy, Pantone 7421 C. Warm, aged, intentional.
4. **Tone-on-tone as highest expression.** Every drop has at least one tonal piece.
5. **Collar as detail.** `REJECT` embroidered on the collar neckband of sleeveless styles.
6. **Puff print for the `R.` monogram.** Raised, textured. Hierarchy: `R.` puff > flat print > flat embroidery.
7. **Angel as founding image.** Cherub, halo, spread wings. The founding graphic motif.
8. **Script lettering as back hero.** Cursive `rejct.all` across the upper back. The brand reveals itself as you leave, not as you arrive.

## 06 — Material Philosophy

> Cotton. Heavy. Honest. Nothing synthetic. — *सूती। भारी। ईमानदार।*

Fabric specifications live in the tech pack. Not on the hang tag. Not in the product description. Not in the brand voice. Materials are described with feeling, not data.

- RJ-001 / RJ-002 — Script Tee: Cotton. 220g. Heavy enough to mean something. Built to outlast the cycle.
- RJ-003 — Loop Tee: Cotton. Heavy jersey. The embroidery is the garment.
- RJ-004 — Circle Sleeveless: Cotton. Cut without sleeves. Worn without compromise.
- RJ-005 — Angel Sleeveless: Cotton. Pure. The print does the rest.
- RJ-006 — Angel Sleeveless Tonal: Cotton. Off-white base. The graphic hides in plain sight.
- Universal fallback: Cotton. Grown. Unblended. No shortcuts.

## 10 — Instagram & Launch

- Handle: `@rejct.all`
- Grid aesthetic: dark editorial. High contrast. Model editorial, ghost mannequin close-ups, detail shots, text-only posts. Never lifestyle.
- Caption voice: short, declarative, often one or two lines. Hindi and English in the same caption — never one without the other.
- Hashtag policy: no hashtags in caption. First comment only if used at all.

**Launch phases:**

**Phase 1 — Before (2–3 weeks pre-drop).** Coming-soon posts. No product shown. Only the `R.` and the rejection pillars — one per day. Build the idea before the object. Let people wonder.

**Phase 2 — Reveal (1 week pre-drop).** Detail close-ups only. Never the full garment. Thread texture, `REJECT` collar, script curve. Ghost mannequin animations.

**Phase 3 — Drop (drop day).** Full editorial shots. All 6 styles, front and back. Hindi and English caption. Link in bio. No countdown. No hype. Just: it's here.

**Phase 4 — After (post-drop).** Real people wearing it. Repost without comment. Remind once: no restocks. Begin Volume 01 teaser.
