# Press reply template

Per `PLAYBOOK_LIBRARY.md → Playbook 03` and Brand Bible §12 (Q3 2026 allows PR seeding to selected individuals, no paid placement).

The Bible does not default to refusing press. It refuses **paid placement and advertorials** specifically. Editorial coverage from an aligned outlet is on the table.

## Paid placement / advertorial / sponsored post

```
Not a fit. Wishing you well.
```

## Gifting for reach / celebrity seeding

```
Not a fit. Wishing you well.
```

## Aligned editorial outlet requesting a written feature

```
Thank you. We are open to written Q&A with aligned outlets.

What we can share:
— The Brand Bible manifesto and Six Rejections.
— Drop 01 imagery and spec-free product language.
— Answers to your questions in writing.

What we won't do:
— Video or live audio interviews at this stage.
— Product-placement arrangements.
— Sharing tech-pack specifications.

Send the questions and timeline when ready.
```

## Aligned individual (stylist, writer, photographer) asking to seed Drop 01

Per Bible Q3 2026 — `selected individuals only, no paid placement`.

```
Thank you for reaching out. Yes — we'd like to send you a piece from Drop 01.

Please share your address and a size preference (XS — XXL, one genderless system).

No obligation, no brief, no hashtags. Wear it when it feels right.
```

---

## Rules

- Never open with `Hi [Name]!`.
- No exclamation points.
- Do not explain the position in the reply — point them to the Bible / the site.
- Brand name lowercase (`rejct.all`) even in email.
