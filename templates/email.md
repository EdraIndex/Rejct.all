# Email template

Per Brand Bible §02 — **Instagram is the primary channel.** Email is not a named channel at launch and may not exist at all in Year 1. This template is a fallback for transactional and very rare editorial sends if and when email is introduced.

## Transactional — order confirmation

```
Subject: Order confirmed — [SKU] [Name]

Your order is in.

[Line items]

Ships by [date]. Tracking on dispatch.
Prepaid. No COD.
No restock.

rejct.all
```

## Transactional — shipped

```
Subject: Shipped — [SKU] [Name]

On its way.

Tracking: [link]

rejct.all
```

## Editorial (rare — when used, one per quarter maximum)

```
Subject: [Max 5 words. No brackets, no emoji.]

[Opening — a statement, not a greeting.]

[Body — 2 to 4 short paragraphs. What we're sharing, why it matters, nothing more.]

[Closing — one line. A Hindi line, a date, or a refusal. Not a sign-off.]

rejct.all
No restock.
[unsubscribe link]
```

## Rules

- No `Hi [name]` openers.
- No `Shop now` CTAs — link to the piece once, inline.
- No PS.
- No urgency (`only 24 hours left`).
- Emails never sell. They share.
- Brand name lowercase in subject and body.
