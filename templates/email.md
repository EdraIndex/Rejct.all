# Email template

Rejct.all emails are rare. Max one per month. Often none.

## Subject
[Maximum 5 words. No emoji, no brackets, no "[NEW]".]

## Body

```
[Opening line — a statement, not a greeting.]

[Body — 2 to 4 short paragraphs. What we're sharing, why it matters, nothing more.]

[Closing — one line. A Hindi line, a date, or a refusal. Not a sign-off.]
```

## Footer

```
Rejct.all
No restock.
[unsubscribe link]
```

---

## Rules

- No "Hi [name]" openers.
- No "Shop now" CTAs. Link to the piece once, inline.
- No PS.
- No urgency ("only 24 hours left").
- If the email is selling, it is wrong. Emails share. They do not sell.
